Fussy Foodie

You can still be a foodie without liking all foods!

Fussy Foodie makes it easy to find recipes you love with the ingredients you love too!

Read more about our Mission
Likes

These are the foods you have specifically told us you like. Don't worry, you don't have to tell us every food that you like, but we will base some of the recipes you see on these foods that you really like. In some cases they could be the star of the show and in others, the perfect accoumpanyment. We will also show you recipes whic include other ingredients, as long as you haven't said they are a dislike, allergen or not included within your diet.

View / Edit Likes
Dislikes, Allergens and Diet

Dislikes are the foods that you have said that you do not like. As such the recipes that we recommend will not include these items. If we find a recipe that you might like which includes one of these items, we will provide a substitution or make you aware that it can be removed from the recipe.


If you have provided your allegen information or the type of diet you chose to follow, we will not include these ingredients in your recipes.

View / Edit Dislikes


Wildcard

The wildcard is an ingredient which is not in your likes or dislikes. We think it is a great chance to introduce you to new ingredients which you may have not thought to include or maybe not come accross before. Make sure to update us on socials if you find a new found love for one of our Wildcard ingredients or Recipes!
